### A Comprehensive Oblivion Modding Guide

Glory of Cyrodiil is a comprehensive Oblivion modding guide for beginners and veterans alike. All instructions are written in great detail, with the more complex instructions having accompanying pictures. 24/7 support is also available on the [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/DhX5S27). It is fully modular and comes with pre-made conflict resolution and load order. All mods have been carefully picked out to avoid old, inefficient, or broken popular mods. This guide will provide you with the most stable experience possible, along with adding/reworking amazing content/mechanics.
